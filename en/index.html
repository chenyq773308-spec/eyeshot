Fused translucent natural stone connecting building facades, interiors and landscape.

Request samplesView projects
EYESHOT fused translucent natural stone bonds 3mm ultra-thin natural stone with glass through a patented hot-fusion process (GSG structure: Glass-PVB-Stone-PVB-Glass), keeping authentic stone veining while achieving translucency, light weight and safety — for building facades, interior walls, sliding doors and landscape feature walls.
